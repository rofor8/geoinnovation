What BNG Monitor uniquely provides
Community ground-truth
Trained volunteers submit structured habitat condition data — stress, dieback, species health. The evidence layer no satellite or desk assessment can replicate.
Three-layer fusion
Satellite detects change, community verifies on the ground, OS provides the authoritative spatial framework. AI reconciles all three into actionable scores.
Council-affordable
£250/month vs. £10,000/yr (Verna) or £300–800/day (consultants). Built on serverless infrastructure costing <£40/month to run nationally.
30-year capable
BNG obligations last decades. Automated satellite monitoring + growing community data network means the system improves with every year of operation.
